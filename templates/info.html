Product Information & User Guide

Learn how to use each feature of our business management system

Home Dashboard

Purpose: Central hub for your business overview

  • View key business statistics and metrics
  • Quick access to recent activities
  • Add products/services to cart for quick invoicing
  • Create quotes directly from the dashboard
  • Monitor business performance at a glance
Tip: Use the floating cart and quote buttons for quick order creation

Inventory Management

Purpose: Manage your product stock and inventory

  • Add new products with details, pricing, and stock levels
  • Track stock quantities and set low stock alerts
  • Update product information and pricing
  • Export inventory data to CSV/Excel
  • Search and filter products efficiently
  • Bulk operations for multiple products
Tip: Set up low stock alerts to never run out of popular items

Services Management

Purpose: Manage your service offerings

  • Create service packages with descriptions and pricing
  • Set service categories and duration
  • Include services in invoices and quotations
  • Track service performance and popularity
  • Export service data for analysis
Tip: Create service bundles to increase average order value

Invoice Management

Purpose: Create and manage customer invoices

  • Generate professional invoices with company branding
  • Add products and services with automatic calculations
  • Track payment status and due dates
  • Send invoices via email or download as PDF
  • Record partial and full payments
  • Generate invoice reports and analytics
Tip: Use the cart feature from home page for quick invoice creation

Quotation System

Purpose: Create quotes for potential customers

  • Generate detailed quotations with terms and conditions
  • Convert quotes to invoices when accepted
  • Track quote status (pending, accepted, rejected)
  • Set quote validity periods
  • Include detailed product/service descriptions
  • Professional PDF generation with company details
Tip: Follow up on pending quotes to improve conversion rates

Customer Enquiries

Purpose: Manage customer inquiries and leads

  • Record customer inquiries with detailed information
  • Track inquiry status and follow-up actions
  • Upload quotation files when provided
  • Convert inquiries to customers and quotes
  • Maintain inquiry history for reference
  • Set reminders for follow-ups
Tip: Regular follow-ups on enquiries can significantly boost sales

Customer Management

Purpose: Maintain comprehensive customer database

  • Store complete customer contact information
  • Track customer purchase history
  • View customer payment records
  • Generate customer-specific reports
  • Export customer data for marketing
  • Maintain customer notes and preferences
Tip: Use customer history to provide personalized service

Expense Tracking

Purpose: Track business expenses and payments

  • Record all business expenses with categories
  • Track vendor payments and outstanding amounts
  • Add multiple payments for single expenses
  • Generate expense reports for accounting
  • Monitor cash flow and payment status
  • Export expense data for tax purposes
Tip: Regular expense tracking helps in better financial planning

Business Insights

Admin Only

Purpose: Advanced analytics and business intelligence

  • Comprehensive sales analytics and trends
  • Customer behavior analysis and growth metrics
  • Inventory performance and category insights
  • Payment method distribution analysis
  • Enquiry conversion rate tracking
  • Expense analysis and vendor performance
  • Interactive charts and visual reports
  • Monthly and yearly comparison data
Tip: Use insights to identify trends and make data-driven business decisions

Admin Features

Admin Only

Purpose: System administration and user management

  • Profile Management: Update personal information and settings
  • Change Password: Secure password management
  • User Management: Create, edit, and manage user accounts
  • Database Management: Backup, restore, and maintain data
  • Role-based Access: Control user permissions and access levels
  • System Monitoring: Track user activities and system health
Tip: Regular database backups ensure data safety and business continuity
